Barista

We are looking for a skilled and friendly Barista to join our team at The Perfect Brew. As a Barista, you'll be responsible for crafting delicious coffee drinks and providing exceptional customer service.

  • Prepare and serve a variety of coffee beverages.
  • Interact with customers, take orders, and provide recommendations.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the coffee bar area.
  • Handle cash transactions and operate the cash register.

Hourly Wage: $12.00

Café Manager

We are seeking an experienced Café Manager to oversee the daily operations of our coffee shop. As a Café Manager, you'll be responsible for managing staff, inventory, and ensuring a delightful customer experience.

  • Supervise and train café staff, including baristas and servers.
  • Manage inventory, order supplies, and maintain stock levels.
  • Create employee schedules and ensure efficient staffing.
  • Handle customer feedback and resolve any issues or concerns.

Salary Range: $35,000 - $40,000 per year
